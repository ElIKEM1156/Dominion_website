About Us

Dominion Children Academy is a vibrant and nurturing environment where young minds are inspired to grow, learn, and achieve excellence. We believe every child is a unique gift and we strive to develop their full potential through quality education, discipline, and moral values.

Founded with a passion for shaping the future, Dominion Children Academy has been committed to providing top-notch education in a safe, welcoming, and stimulating atmosphere. Our experienced and caring teachers work closely with students to prepare them academically, socially, and spiritually for the challenges ahead.

Admissions

We are excited to welcome new students into the Dominion Children Academy family!

Enrollment is open for all classes from Nursery to Junior High School.

Admission Requirements:

  • Completed application form (available at the school office)
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Previous school records (if any)
  • Passport-sized photographs

Admission Period: January, May, and September every year.

Visit our school today or call us for more information!
